Description

Team TAIYO ZEN has twenty five engineers with the goal to build and race electric powered car. The team has designed a four wheeled electric vehicle with innovative ideas that tackle the challenge of designing a fully optimized and usable vehicle for todayâ€™s market. As technology continues to be refined and advanced; we hope that platforms such as the Taiyo-Zen vehicle can be used as benchmarks for furthering design and innovation.

The current world situation is that we are running out of fossil fuels as well as CLEAN air. To tackle this issue we decided to design an eco-friendly electric car which has performance comparative to current on road IC engine cars and is affordable by a middle class family. For that purpose we designed a two seater rear wheel drive vehicle. The vehicle is powered by a BLDC motor coupled with a gearbox for power transmission. For higher efficiency and endurance Li-ion batteries is used as a source of power for the motor. The car has a ground clearance of 7 inch to work without any hitch on Indian roads and has independent suspension on all four wheels. For safer and more efficient braking disc brakes are being used in the vehicle on all four wheels plus one additional parking brake.

The car has max speed of 100kmph and can run up to 105km on a single charge (@75kmph average speed). After the battery has been fully discharged it will only take 2hrs to fully charge once plugged in or four hrs via solar.

For driver comfort power steering has been installed along with a very spacious driver cabinet with enough leg space for the driver as well as the passenger. The car is coupled with modern digital dashboard on which all the parameters of the car can be viewed, also the same can be viewed on any other phone via a simple app.
